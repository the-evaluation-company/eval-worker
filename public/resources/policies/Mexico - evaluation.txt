Purpose:
This policy guides evaluators or automated systems (e.g., Gemini 2.0 Flash) to determine the U.S. equivalency of educational credentials from Mexico.

Best OCR Results Guidance (for LLM - Gemini 2.0 Flash):
- Ensure uploaded documents are clear, high-resolution (minimum 300 dpi).
- Documents should be aligned, free of shadows or distortion, and preferably digitally-issued PDFs.

Key Definitions:
- SEP: Secretaría de Educación Pública (Mexican Federal Education Authority)
- RVOE: Reconocimiento de Validez Oficial de Estudios (Recognition required for private institutions)
- Título: Official diploma or degree certificate awarded upon graduation
- Pasante: Status of having completed coursework without final degree award

MULTI-PART QUALIFICATION HANDLING:
Some Mexican qualifications appear as separate phrases in the document that should be combined:
- "CERTIFICADO DE TERMINACIÓN DE ESTUDIOS" + "LOS ESTUDIOS CORRESPONDIENTES AL BACHILLERATO GENERAL"
  should be combined as "Certificado de Terminación de Estudios - Bachillerato General"
- "TÍTULO DE LICENCIADO" + programOfStudy name should be combined as "Título de Licenciado en [programOfStudy]"
- Look for phrases separated by institutional text or spanning multiple sections

Document Identification and Evaluation IF-THEN-ELSE Structure:

Step 1: Verification of Document Authenticity and Institutional Accreditation

IF document clearly includes:
- Official SEP accreditation number or RVOE for private institutions
- Official institutional signatures/stamps
- Unique document or folio number
THEN proceed to credential-level identification
ELSE request additional documentation or verification from issuing institution

Step 2: Credential-Level Identification and Equivalency Determination:

IF Document is "Certificado de Bachillerato":
- Typical Duration: 3 years post-secondary
- THEN equivalency = "Diploma from an accredited senior high school"

ELSE IF Document is "Título de Técnico Superior Universitario / Profesional Asociado":
- Typical Duration: 2 years post-bachillerato
- THEN equivalency = "Associate degree from a regionally-accredited institution of higher education"

ELSE IF Document is "Título de Licenciado en [programOfStudy]" OR "Ingeniero en [programOfStudy]":
- THEN output: "Bachelor's degree in [programOfStudyEnglishName] from a regionally-accredited institution of higher education"

ELSE IF Document is a Licenciatura-level in Law, Medicine, Dentistry, Veterinary Medicine, Pharmacy, Chiropractic Medicine:
- THEN output: "Bachelor's degree and first professional degree in [law/medicine/dentistry/veterinary medicine/pharmacy/chiropractic medicine] from a regionally-accredited institution of higher education"

ELSE IF Document is "Título de Maestría en [programOfStudy]":
- THEN output: "Master's degree in [programOfStudyEnglishName] from a regionally-accredited institution of higher education"

ELSE IF Document is "Título de Doctorado en [programOfStudy]":
- THEN output: "Doctoral degree (Ph.D.) in [programOfStudyEnglishName] from a regionally-accredited institution of higher education"

ELSE IF Document indicates partial studies ("Pasante"):
- THEN output: "Partial undergraduate study in [programOfStudyEnglishName] at a regionally-accredited institution of higher education. No degree awarded."

ELSE:
- THEN escalate to senior evaluator or require additional documentation

Step 3: Special Disclaimers for Regulated Professions
For credentials in regulated programOfStudys (e.g., Law, Medicine, Dentistry, Veterinary Medicine, Pharmacy, Chiropractic Medicine):
- Include: "This evaluation confirms academic equivalency only. Professional practice or licensure in the United States may require additional certification or examinations."

Evaluator's Quick Reference:
- High School (Bachillerato): Diploma from an accredited senior high school
- Technical/Associate (TSU): Associate degree from a regionally-accredited institution
- Bachelor's (Licenciatura): Bachelor's degree in [programOfStudyEnglishName] from a regionally-accredited institution of higher education
- First Professional (Licenciatura-level, professional programOfStudys): Bachelor's degree and first professional degree in [programOfStudy] from a regionally-accredited institution of higher education
- Master's (Maestría): Master's degree in [programOfStudyEnglishName] from a regionally-accredited institution of higher education
- Doctoral (Doctorado): Doctoral degree (Ph.D.) in [programOfStudyEnglishName]
